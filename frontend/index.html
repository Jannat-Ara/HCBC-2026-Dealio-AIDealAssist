Agent Pipeline Reference

①
OrchestratorLlama 3.3 70B

Decomposes the business objective into structured subtasks. Always runs first.

IN Free-form objective text
OUT List of subtasks (title + description)
②
LearnerLlama 3.1 8B

Searches the KB across all active domains (RAG). Produces a feasibility report with evidence and gaps.

IN Subtasks + KB search results
OUT Feasibility score, confidence, gaps
③
Decision MakerLlama 3.3 70B

Generates a formal business recommendation with risk level and executive summary.

IN Learner feasibility report
OUT Recommendation + risk + summary
⏸
Human-in-the-Loop GateRequired

Pipeline pauses here. Go to Reports tab to Approve or Reject before any tasks are created.

IN Your decision + optional feedback
OUT Approved → Task Gen / Rejected → re-loops
④
Task GeneratorLlama 3.1 8B

Creates 2–4 department tasks with instructions, deadlines, and dependencies dispatched to Celery queues.

IN Approved decision + your feedback
OUT Department tasks → Redis → Celery
